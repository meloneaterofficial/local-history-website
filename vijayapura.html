Welcome to Vijayapura

The foundation of this historic city was laid during the reign of the Chalukyan dynasty of Kalyani between the 10th and 11th centuries.

They called it VIJAYAPURA or the City of Victory. Vijayapura experienced a great burst of architectural activity under the Adil Shahi dynasty.

The Adil Shahis encouraged building activity to such an extent that Vijayapura itself has over 50 mosques, more than 20 tombs and a number of palaces. Vijayapura is mainly famous for Gol-Gumbaz, Jumma Masjid, Bara Kamaan, Gajanan Temple, Ibrahim Roza,Taj Bawdi, Malik-e-Maidan, Methar Mahal, Gagan Mahal, Jala Manzil, Upli Burj, Shivagiri, Siddeshwar Temple etc.

LOCAL LORE

Deep within the ancient city of Vijayapura lies a story whispered by the elders. It’s said that centuries ago, during the reign of a just and virtuous ruler, a magnificent jewel adorned the crown worn by the king. This jewel, known as the “Crown Gem,” was believed to possess mystical properties, bringing prosperity and harmony to the kingdom. The people of Vijayapura held it in high esteem, attributing the city’s golden era to the power of the Crown Gem. As time went on, a shadow fell upon the kingdom as the Crown Gem mysteriously disappeared from the king’s crown. The people searched far and wide, but the jewel remained elusive. Legend has it that a humble potter, on the outskirts of Vijayapura, dreamt of the missing gem. Guided by his dreams, he embarked on a quest that led him through treacherous terrains and ancient temples. After years of searching, the potter stumbled upon a hidden chamber beneath the city’s grandest mosque. There, surrounded by forgotten relics, he found the Crown Gem resting on a pedestal of pure marble. With reverence, the potter returned the Crown Gem to the king, and the city flourished once more. The potter’s humility and devotion became a symbol of Vijayapura’s resilience and the belief that even the most humble can play a significant role in shaping destiny. To this day, the people of Vijayapura share the tale of the Lost Crown Jewel, a reminder that within their history lies stories of valor, determination, and the enduring power of unity.
